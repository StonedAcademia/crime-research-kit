## Source Independence Notes

- Senate, CIA IG, Rockefeller, House, FBI, and National Security Archive
  records are different source groups, but later sources may quote or summarize
  earlier records. Do not count repeated text as independent corroboration.
- National Security Archive context is valuable for discovery and collection
  history, but claim confidence should rise only when primary records or
  independent official findings support the same point.
- House witness PDFs are official hearing exhibits, yet the content is witness
  testimony. Store the speaker, date, and testimony status on each claim.
