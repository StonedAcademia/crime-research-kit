# Privacy Redaction Rules

Block public export for:

- Living private people without clear public-interest need.
- Minors and juvenile records.
- Home addresses, private workplaces, phone/email, precise private coordinates, and contact details.
- Medical/mental-health details.
- Financial identifiers, account numbers, SSNs, student IDs, license identifiers not needed for public context.
- Non-public relatives and family details.
- Weak allegations, lead-only claims, and unsupported private claims.
- Sealed, exempt, expunged, or unlawfully obtained material.

Use `redactions.jsonl` to record what was removed or excluded and why.
