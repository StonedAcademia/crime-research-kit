# FOIA And Open-Records Request Checklist

## Scope Fields

- Agency/public body and jurisdiction.
- Subject and public-interest purpose.
- Date range.
- Record categories and likely custodians.
- Preferred format and electronic delivery request.
- Fee estimate or fee-waiver language when appropriate.
- Privacy exclusions for private-person details, minors, medical details, financial identifiers, and non-responsive private material.

## Response Tracking

Track:

- Submitted date.
- Tracking number.
- Statutory due date.
- Extension date.
- Fee estimate.
- Response status.
- Appeal due date.
- Released record source IDs.

## Intake Rules

Responsive records become evidence only after they are registered as sources, preserved, extracted, and linked through source IDs and source spans.
