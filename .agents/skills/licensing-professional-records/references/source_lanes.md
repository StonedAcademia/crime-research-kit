# Licensing And Professional Source Lanes

Use official board and regulator records first.

## Official Records

- State professional licensing boards and lookup portals.
- Disciplinary orders, consent agreements, board minutes, sanctions, revocations, suspensions, probation notices, and reinstatement orders.
- National or specialist certification bodies where certification status is public.
- Court/regulator records tied to professional discipline or eligibility.
- Public institutional profiles when they state credentials or roles.

## Secondary Sources

- Reputable reporting that links to or quotes official board records.
- Professional association pages, employer bios, conference programs, publications, or self-reported profiles as context only.
- Commercial credential databases or people-search pages as leads only.

## Source Grading Defaults

- `A`: official board/regulator/license/court record or public disciplinary order.
- `B`: reputable reporting or institutional record with clear sourcing.
- `C`: self-reported biography, employer profile, professional association page, or conference bio.
- `D`: commercial database, social profile, scraped listing, forum/wiki, or same-name lookup.
- `X`: AI summary, unverifiable content, private/doxxing material, or fabricated/provenance-free source.
