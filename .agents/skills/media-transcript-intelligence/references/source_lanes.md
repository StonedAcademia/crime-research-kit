# Media And Transcript Source Lanes

Use original media and official transcripts first.

## Strong Sources

- Original public video/audio URL and publisher metadata.
- Official transcript, caption file, hearing transcript, deposition transcript, broadcast transcript, or public archive.
- Documentary/interview pages with date, producer, publisher, and speaker context.
- Court/government hearing recordings and transcripts.

## Secondary Or Lead Sources

- Clips, excerpts, reposts, fan transcripts, social-media captions, commentary videos, and autogenerated captions.
- Search snippets or AI-generated summaries are not evidence.

## Source Grading Defaults

- `A`: official transcript, public hearing transcript, original public media from official source, court/government recording.
- `B`: reputable publisher transcript, documentary, broadcast archive, or interview with clear metadata.
- `C`: memoir/interview context, self-published media, autogenerated captions needing review.
- `D`: clips, reposts, fan transcripts, social posts, uncited commentary, or edited excerpts without provenance.
- `X`: AI summaries, unverifiable/fabricated content, private recordings without permission, or unlawfully obtained media.
